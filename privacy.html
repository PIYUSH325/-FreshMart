Privacy
Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we protect your information.

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to FreshMart ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website freshmart.com, use our services, or interact with us in any other way.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name: Provided during registration or checkout
  • Email address: For account creation and communication
  • Phone number: For delivery coordination
  • Delivery address: For order fulfillment
  • Payment information: Processed securely by third-party gateways (we don't store card details)

2.2 Order Information

  • Purchased items and quantities
  • Order history and delivery details
  • Special instructions or preferences

2.3 Usage Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: Processing orders, arranging delivery, customer service
  • Communication: Order updates, promotional offers (with opt-out), support
  • Service Improvement: Analytics, feedback analysis, feature development
  • Marketing: Personalized recommendations and offers (with consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Fraud prevention, regulatory requirements

4. Information Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Delivery Partners: Name, phone, address for delivery only
  • Payment Processors: Payment details for transaction processing
  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, customer support (under strict agreements)
  • Law Enforcement: When required by law

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Two-factor authentication for accounts

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request data removal
  • Objection: Object to marketing communications
  • Portability: Receive data in structured format

Contact us at privacy@freshmart.com to exercise these rights.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies for improvement. You can manage preferences in browser settings. Third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics (website analytics)
  • Facebook Pixel (advertising)

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Data Transfers

Data processed in India. May be transferred to service providers in EU/USA under GDPR-compliant agreements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes posted here with updated date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
